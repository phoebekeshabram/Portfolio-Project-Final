About Me

Hi, my name is Phoebe, and thank you for viewing my portfolio! I was born and raised in Monterey, California, but currently reside in Vancouver, Washington, with my husband and two children. While working on my BFA, I got married and had two children which ultimately required me to put my career aside for a few years. That is until I learned about Thinkful. My interest in development came about a year ago, after learning about it from my husband, who is an IT Specialist for the Oregon National Guard.

My work in graphic design felt like it was hitting a lot of dead ends, so after some deliberation I decided to enroll in the Thinkful Software Engineering Immersion Program.

I've always had an interest in tech, as early as 10 years old working on my MySpace page layout and design. I have been equally passionate about problem solving and puzzles, so with having a solid skillset in design, I feel confident in the programmer I am becoming. As the tech industry continues to explore unknown possibilities and advances in human life, I am excited to be catching this fast pace ride into the world of software engineering. I know my unique perspective combing the arts, humanities, and technology will be the foundation for many innovative and creative projects to be born.
